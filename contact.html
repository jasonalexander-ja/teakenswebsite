Commission TOS

Below are my Terms of Service regarding any and all of my Commission Services.

I. Keep in mind I have a full time job on top of doing commissions I mostly do commissions in my spare time for fun. It may take 1-3 weeks after you purchase a commission to receive your commission.

II. Upon completion delivery you only get 3 free edits/changes. (case by case basis)

III. Any mistakes of my own doing (wrong color, incorrect markings, etc.) Will be corrected free of charge.

IV. I edit the blend shapes on a case by case basis to match more closely to the Fursona I am working with. So certain markings/textures might be a little off.

V. Once at least 50% of the payment is received work begins on the commissioned piece.

VI. Delivery of avatar will be completed once I have received full payment, payment plans are accepted (message me directly).

VII. PayPal, Venmo, and Cashapp are accepted.

VIII. While I am building your avatar I will upload a public avatar of your commission for you to walk around in and see if there are any issues and or mistakes.

IX. I retain the right to use your texture as an example of my work for future clients. All avatars on my account will be set to private and I will not claim ownership.

X. NSFW is available as long as certain criteria is met.

XI. I will not upload a NSFW avatar to my personal account EVER. A NSFW commission requires account information to be provided when completed, OR the entire project can be sent VIA Google Drive As long as you own all of the base assets you are requesting.

XII. If you choose to upload your avatar yourself, I assume you know what you are doing. Any help will require a service fee. (case by case)

XIII. You are not allowed to sell my work or pass it off as your own, Please be sure to credit me as the one responsible for your avatar being brought into VRChat.

XIV. Keep in mind I work when motivation hits me. Slot number does not mean completion order.

XV. You MUST be 18+ to commission me any sort of NSFW project. I refuse to do NSFW Projects for anyone under the age of 18.

XVI. Trades are no longer an acceptable form of payment due to issues in the past.

By commissioning me you understand and accept and agree to all of the terms stated in this document.
