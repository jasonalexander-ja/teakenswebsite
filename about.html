About Me

Hello My name is Danni but I go by Teaken. I am a female to male transgender my pronouns are She/Her.
I have always had an artistic side but when it came to pen and paper I lacked skill.
On a whim I bought 3D painting software and my first 3D VRchat avatar and started recoloring them.
First I would do friends for free just to get practice and it took one of my friends saying hun you are really good you should start doing commissions... Well here we are!
i have been doing commissions going on a year now from simple and easy to complicated and extravagant. Please feel free to inquire I am friendly and do not bite.
